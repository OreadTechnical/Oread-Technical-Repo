About Oread Technical

Founded by Jeremy deNoyelles in 2008, Oread Technical provides multi-discipline aerospace engineering support across all phases of aircraft development — from preliminary design through fleet sustainment.

With leadership experience managing teams of up to 14 engineers and deep hands-on expertise in metal and composite structures, Oread Technical helps programs move from analysis to released drawings with confidence.

Services

End-to-end structural engineering support for demanding aerospace programs.

01

Classical Stress Analysis

Static and fatigue analysis of metal and composite airframe structures, from preliminary sizing through formal documentation and MRB support.

02

Finite Element Modeling

FEM development and survey using NASTRAN, PATRAN, ANSYS, and FEMAP for load path verification, buckling, and fleet issue resolution.

03

Analysis Tool Development

Excel-based pre-processors, optimization routines, and composite laminate theory tools that accelerate analyst workflows and reduce rework.

04

Program Leadership

Team management, SOW/BOE development, design review presentations, supplier coordination, and earned value management as a certified CAM.

05

Composite Structures

Analysis of frames, stringers, nacelle and thrust reverser structures using customer and in-house tools including ISAMI and laminate theory methods.

06

Fleet & Modification Support

Structural support for conversions, flight test installations, cargo modifications, and ongoing fleet sustainment programs.
